  Ultra-realistic cinematic male portrait — Blender Cycles render
CAMERA & COMPOSITION
Vertical close to medium portrait framed from chest to head. Camera positioned at eye level inside a car, creating a natural and intimate perspective. Subject centered in frame. Sharp subject focus with shallow depth of field, softly blurring background elements inside and outside the vehicle.
POSE & BODY POSITIONING
Seated in the driver’s seat with torso facing forward. One shoulder slightly closer to the camera, creating a subtle natural angle. Seatbelt crossing diagonally over the chest, adding realism and context. Posture upright and relaxed.
HEAD POSITION & GAZE
Head slightly tilted downward and subtly angled forward. Eyes looking directly at the camera, creating a strong and engaging connection. Neck naturally aligned, enhancing presence.
FACIAL EXPRESSION
Neutral confident expression with relaxed facial muscles. Lips closed with a slight firmness. Expression conveys calm, control, and quiet intensity.
FACE & IDENTITY
Young adult man with strong masculine facial structure. Defined jawline, straight nose bridge, and balanced proportions. Prominent mustache combined with short beard stubble, adding character and realism. Subtle skin texture with natural tonal variation.
HAIR
Short neutral hair adapted from reference identity, mostly covered by a fitted black cap. Visible hair edges maintain realistic density and natural texture.
ACCESSORIES & DETAILS
Black baseball cap with minimal logo detail. Small ear piercing visible. Seatbelt crossing torso. No excessive accessories, maintaining a clean and grounded aesthetic.
OUTFIT
Fitted gray t-shirt with soft fabric texture, slightly stretched across the chest and shoulders. Casual and minimal styling appropriate for everyday wear.
BODY & PHYSIQUE
Athletic masculine physique with broad shoulders and defined upper body structure visible through the shirt. Natural proportions preserved without exaggeration.
SKIN & REALISM
Ultra-detailed skin rendering with visible pores, subtle color variation, and natural warm undertones. Slight natural imperfections enhancing realism. Physically accurate subsurface scattering and realistic skin response typical of Blender Cycles rendering.
LIGHTING
Natural daylight entering from the side window, creating soft directional lighting across the face. Gentle highlights on cheekbones and forehead with mild shadowing adding depth. Balanced exposure with realistic light falloff.
ENVIRONMENT
Car interior with visible seats, window, and urban street elements outside. Background includes blurred vehicles, buildings, and greenery, reinforcing a real-world setting.
MOOD & AESTHETIC
Modern casual lifestyle aesthetic — calm, confident, and grounded. Cinematic atmosphere emphasizing realism and authenticity.
STYLE & RENDER QUALITY
Ultra-photorealistic Blender Cycles render, cinematic realism, 8K resolution, HDR, razor-sharp focus, realistic skin micro-detail, physically accurate lighting, IMVU / Second Life compatible realism, reference-based identity preservation, no stylization or artistic exaggeration.
